Report a barrier

Let us know about a barrier that's affecting UK companies exporting or importing.

You can save your information and come back later to complete.

You will be asked:

  • to describe the barrier
  • what the status of the barrier is
  • which countries the barrier relates to
  • whether the barrier affects exporting or importing
  • which sectors the barrier affects
  • which companies the barrier affects
  • which goods and services or investments the barrier affects
  • whether the barrier can be published on GOV.UK
  • to provide a title and summary that are suitable for the public to read
