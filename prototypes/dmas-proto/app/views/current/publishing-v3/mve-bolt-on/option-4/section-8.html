Barrier publication

{{ govukFieldset({ legend: { text: "Can the barrier be published on GOV.UK?", classes: "govuk-fieldset__legend--s", isPageHeading: true } }) }}

All market access barriers should be published unless there is a valid reason not to.

{% set listOfExemptions %}

A barrier should not be published if:

  • the company affected could be easily identified, even once the company name has been removed
  • the barrier was shared with the government in confidence
  • publishing the barrier might damage the UK’s relationship with the host government
  • it is defence or security related
  • there are specific strategic concerns relating to upcoming negotiations
  • it affects imports into the UK
  • it is a future or potential barrier that is not yet affecting trade
  • an other government department (OGD) or ?? (DA) has advised that it should not be published
