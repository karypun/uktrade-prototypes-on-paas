Obtaining your certificate: step by step

An explanation of how to go about obtaining an export certificate using this service.

  1. Step 1: Check if you need a certificate

    Check if you need a certificate based on destination country, certificate type and goods type.

    Read the resulting guidance to understand what you need to do.

  2. Step 2: Create an account

    Create an account to Apply for an export certificate.

    Create an account

  3. Step 3: Apply for an export certificate

    Sign into your account to apply for certificates, manage existing applications or renew certificates.

    Sign into your account

    Create an account

  4. Step 4: Apply for a certificate

    Complete the form accurately, submit required documents and track the progress. Await the outcome after review. Promptly respond to requests for additional information, if needed..

  5. Step 5: Issuance, rejection or renewal

    You will receive an approval, a detailed explanation for a rejection or a a renewal to ensure smooth import/export operations.
