Obtaining your licence or certificate: step by step

  1. Step 1: Check if you need a licence or a certificate

    Check licence or certificate requirements based on type, origin, destination, and goods.

  2. Step 2: Read the guidance applicable to your goods

    Read the guidance for your goods to understand the licensing and certification requirements. Stay updated to comply with regulations and ensure smooth import/export processes.

  3. Step 3: Sign in or create an account

    The account will allow you to manage applications, add a colleague to your account and access support.

  4. Step 4: Apply for a licence or a certificate

    Complete the form accurately, submit required documents and track the progress. Await the outcome after review. Promptly respond to requests for additional information, if needed..

  5. Step 5: Issuance, rejection or renewal

    You will receive an approval, a detailed explanation for a rejection or a a renewal to ensure smooth import/export operations.
